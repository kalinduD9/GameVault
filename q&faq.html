Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For in-store purchases, we also accept cash and debit cards.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened items in their original packaging. For opened items, we offer a 7-day return policy if the product is defective.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please check our shipping calculator at checkout for specific details.
Are your games new or pre-owned?
We offer both new and pre-owned games. All pre-owned games are thoroughly tested and guaranteed to work. The condition is clearly marked on each product page.
